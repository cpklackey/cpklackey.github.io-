Projects

Jeopardy Game Show Analysis and Study Guide

As a fan of Jeopardy, if one was to prepare to go on the show or just wanted to practice being better at it, how could one devise a study plan? If you had all the questions and answers for every Jeopardy show, that would be useful. However, attempting to study every question from every show one by one could take a very long time.

Utilizing NLP tokenizing, topic modeling, and YAKE keyword extraction, this project analyzed 300,000 plus questions from 35 years of Jeopardy data and a model was developed to construct the most frequently used keywords for the most frequently occurring answers.

Temperature Change Forecast

The Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations is a specialized agency of the United Nations that leads international efforts to defeat hunger and improve nutrition and food security. Increases in surface air temperature associated with rising greenhouse gas concentrations threaten plant growth and yield, putting millions of farmers and communities at risk throughout the world.

This project focused on building a time series model to analyze and forecast temperature change using a dataset from FAOSTAT. A sarimax model was built and tuned based on seasonality and trends to simulate changes in temperature both globally and for subregions of the earth.

Restaurant Revenue Price Prediction

This project aims to model and predict revenue on quick restaurant services in Europe and Asia. Such a model would be valuable in helping to decide where to build new restaurants.

A machine learning model was created to predict revenue utilizing regularization tools such as Lasso and Ridge, and by testing regression models including random forest, support vectors, KNN, decision trees, bagging, and boosting.
